# International Invoicing (Facturation internationale)

## Legal basis

- CGI: Articles 259 to 259 D (place of supply rules)
- CGI: Article 262 (export exemption)
- CGI: Article 283-2 (reverse charge)
- EU VAT Directive 2006/112/EC

## Intra-EU B2B — Goods

### Rules
- Seller does NOT charge VAT (exemption art. 262 ter-I du CGI)
- Buyer applies reverse charge in their country
- Both parties must have valid intra-EU VAT numbers

### Mandatory invoice mentions
- Buyer's intra-EU VAT number
- Seller's intra-EU VAT number
- "Exonération de TVA — article 262 ter-I du CGI"

### Declarative obligations
- **DEB (Déclaration d'Échanges de Biens)** — now replaced by:
  - **EMEBI** (Enquête Mensuelle sur les Échanges de Biens Intra-UE)
    for statistical purposes (shipments > 460,000 EUR/year)
  - **État récapitulatif TVA** on the CA3 VAT declaration
- Filed monthly before the 10th of the following month

## Intra-EU B2B — Services

### General rule (Art. 259-1° CGI)
- Services taxable where the buyer is established
- Seller does NOT charge French VAT
- Buyer applies reverse charge

### Mandatory invoice mentions
- Buyer's intra-EU VAT number
- "Autoliquidation — article 283-2 du CGI"

### Declarative obligations
- **DES (Déclaration Européenne de Services)**
- Filed monthly before the 10th of the following month
- Reports the value of services provided to EU B2B clients

## Intra-EU B2C

### Goods — Distance selling
- VAT charged at **buyer's country rate** when above 10,000 EUR/year
  threshold (all EU countries combined)
- Below threshold: French VAT applies
- **OSS (One-Stop Shop)** can be used for simplified declarations

### Services — General
- French VAT applies (services taxable where supplier is established)
- Exception: digital services → taxable where consumer is located
  (use OSS/IOSS for simplified filing)

## Export outside EU — Goods

### Rules
- **Full VAT exemption** (art. 262-I du CGI)
- Must have proof of export (customs declaration, DAU/EX)
- Goods must physically leave EU territory

### Mandatory invoice mentions
- "Exonération de TVA — article 262-I du CGI"
- No need for buyer VAT number (non-EU)

### Declarative obligations
- Customs export declaration
- No DEB/DES for non-EU exports

## Export outside EU — Services

### General rule
- Services to non-EU B2B clients: NOT subject to French VAT
  (Art. 259-1° CGI — place of supply is buyer's country)
- "TVA non applicable — article 259-1 du CGI"

### Exceptions
- Services related to immovable property in France: French VAT applies
- Cultural, artistic, sporting services performed in France: French VAT applies

## Language considerations

- Invoices to foreign clients may be issued in a language other than French
- However, the French tax authority may request a French translation
  during an audit
- Best practice: bilingual invoice (French + client's language)
