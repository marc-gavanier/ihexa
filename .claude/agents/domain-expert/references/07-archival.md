# Archival and Conservation (Archivage et conservation)

## Legal basis

- Code de commerce: Article L123-22
- Livre des procédures fiscales: Article L102 B
- CGI: Article 289

## Conservation periods

| Document type                  | Duration     | Starting from      |
|--------------------------------|--------------|--------------------|
| Invoices (issued and received) | **10 years** | End of fiscal year |
| Accounting books and records   | **10 years** | End of fiscal year |
| Commercial correspondence      | **5 years**  | Date of document   |
| Bank statements                | **5 years**  | Date of statement  |
| Tax declarations               | **6 years**  | Year of filing     |
| Social security documents      | **5 years**  | Date of document   |

**Recommendation**: retain invoices for **10 years** even though the
fiscal control period is 6 years. The commercial obligation is 10 years.

## Format requirements

### Paper invoices
- Must be stored in original form
- Can be digitized for practical use, but original must be kept
  UNLESS the digitization meets NF Z42-026 standard

### Electronic invoices
- Must be stored in their **original electronic format**
- Cannot be printed and stored as paper only
- Must guarantee: authenticity, integrity, readability throughout
  the entire conservation period

### Digitized paper invoices (NF Z42-026)
- If paper invoices are scanned and stored digitally:
  - Scan must be identical to the original
  - Must use NF Z42-026 compliant process
  - Original paper can then be destroyed
  - Digital copy becomes the legal reference

## Integrity guarantees

The stored invoice must demonstrate:
- **Authenticity of origin**: the identity of the issuer is guaranteed
- **Integrity of content**: the content has not been altered
- **Readability**: the invoice remains readable throughout conservation

Three accepted methods:
1. **Qualified electronic signature** (signature électronique qualifiée)
2. **EDI** (Electronic Data Interchange) with audit trail
3. **Reliable audit trail** (piste d'audit fiable) — documenting the
   link between the invoice and the underlying transaction

## Sanctions

| Violation                                  | Sanction                          |
|--------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| Unable to produce invoices on request      | Up to **10,000 EUR**              |
| Voluntary destruction or prolonged absence | Up to **75,000 EUR**              |
| Missing invoices during tax audit          | Presumption of undeclared revenue |

## Right of access

The tax authority can request access to all invoices during:
- **Fiscal audit** (vérification de comptabilité): up to 6 years back
- **Right of communication** (droit de communication): 6 years
- **Tax recovery** (recouvrement): up to 10 years in some cases

Invoices must be produced within a reasonable timeframe (typically
15 days for a formal request).
